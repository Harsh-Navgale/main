Event Details

HICAThon 1.0 is an inaugural hacker summit organized by the HICA club, scheduled for 20 and 21 March. This event will bring together cybersecurity professionals, enthusiasts, and students from across India to engage in knowledge-sharing, skill-building, and networking opportunities. The summit will include expert sessions, panel discussions, and an 8-hour Capture the Flag (CTF) competition.

March 20th Schedule

Expert Session

Join leading cybersecurity experts as they explore emerging trends, best practices, and cutting-edge solutions shaping the future of the industry. Gain valuable insights into the latest developments and strategies for navigating the evolving cybersecurity landscape.

Panel Discussion

Engage with a panel of distinguished experts in a thought-provoking discussion on critical cybersecurity challenges and future directions. This session will delve into key issues, offering diverse perspectives on how the industry is adapting and innovating to stay ahead of emerging threats.

March 21st - CTF Competition

Capture The Flag

A challenging 8-hour Capture the Flag event designed to test and enhance participants' problem-solving and cybersecurity skills.
